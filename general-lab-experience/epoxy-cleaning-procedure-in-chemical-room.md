# Epoxy Cleaning Procedure in Chemical Room

* Wear gloves
* Place warming container in the cleaning desk, pour in some water
* Connect the warming container to power and then warming it for a few mins
* Place glass beaker into the water bath of warming container
* Add Acetone to clean glass beaker and soak object for 15 minutes.
* Using tweezer take out object and pour Acetone into waste container
* Add isopropanol to clean glass beaker and soak object for 15 minutes.
* Take out object and pour Isopropanol into waste container
* Use dryer to dry object, wrap it in foil paper
* Dispose of water in warming container into sink
* Clean up
